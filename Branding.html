    • Veneman en de Groot

    This semester we started with Fast Forward introduction week. We needed to come up with an idea together in the field of ICT that could come true in 2050. We had a week for this. On Friday we talked to various teachers and students about our idea.

    Moodboard

    For this project, we worked on the branding for a singer and a female singer who will be doing a pub crawl in the brown caf√©s of Eindhoven this upcoming April. Woody gave us a presentation about who he and Truus de Groot are. He also showed us some videos of what they have done together so far. From that presentation, I got the impression that they sing smartlappen (Dutch sentimental songs) for an older audience in brown pubs. I based my first mood board on this impression, incorporating brown pubs, dark colors, and musical notes into the design.
